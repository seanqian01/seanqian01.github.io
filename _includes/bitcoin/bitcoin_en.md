##### Cryptocurrency Exchanges

|----- | -----|-----|
|[Binance][1] | Claims to be the largest trading volume platform | Founded by Chinese, friendly to Chinese users |
|[OKX][3] | Web3 wallet is a favorite among airdrop hunters | Also founded by Chinese |
|[Bybit][4] | Self-proclaimed world's second largest | Operations team based in the Netherlands |
|[NEXO][5] | Decent trading volume | European-oriented |
|[Huobi][6] | Founded by Chinese | Home for Chinese traders |
|[Coinbase][7] | World's largest exchange, US listed company | Established exchange, currently not serving Chinese users |

|[Hyperliquid][30] | Decentralized exchange | HFT quantitative team background, very popular in 2024, fast trading speed, lower fees compared to traditional DEXs |
|[Uniswap][31] | Mainstream and early-established decentralized exchange | Traditional decentralized exchange |
